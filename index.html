1.NEED FOR SECURITY

Basic Concepts

Computer security consists of measures to protect hardware, software, information and people. It has always been crucial, since any failure can have serious economic, social or personal consequences.

Big Data

Management and analysis of large volumes of data, which implies new risks and the need to better protect user privacy.

Internet of Things

Connecting everyday objects to the Internet, which increases the number of devices and people connected, and therefore the risks.

6.THREATS AND FRAUDS AGAINST PEOPLE

  • Scam SMS and calls
  • Identity theft

Social engineering

  • Psychological manipulation
  • Abuse of confidence
  • emotional blackmail

Common Scams

  • Too good offers
  • Romantic scams
  • Fake investments

Protective Measures

  • Always verify the identity of the sender
  • Do not share personal or banking information
  • Be wary of incredible offers
  • Contact authorities if you have any suspicions
